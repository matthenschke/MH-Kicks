About Us

Welcome to M.H Kicks

Since 2020, M.H. Kicks has been truly committed to provide individuals with their favorite sneakers. Matt Henschke has been dedicated throughout his entire life to collect every sneaker that was ever created and worn. Now as a retired sneaker collector, he looks to give back to all sneaker lovers out there! Come to the different locations on Staten Island to see all of his shoes for yourself!

Which started off as a pipe dream became a reality for Matt himself. He always envisioned creating a sneaker franchise that would make sneakers that would cost hundreds or even thousands more affordable. All sneakers are 100% authentic from Matt's very own sneaker closet. Our vision is to provide everyone, even non-sneaker lovers with high quality and affordable shoes, bringing their fashion and style to the next level.
